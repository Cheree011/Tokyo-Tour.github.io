Basic Conversation

Context: Two friends meeting after a long time.
A: こんにちは、久しぶりですね！
B: こんにちは、本当に久しぶりですね。元気でしたか？
A: はい、元気でした。あなたは？
B: 私も元気でした。最近どうですか？
A: 忙しいけど、楽しいです。

Translation:
A: Konnichiwa, hisashiburi desu ne!
B: Konnichiwa, hontou ni hisashiburi desu ne. Genki deshita ka?
A: Hai, genki deshita. Anata wa?
B: Watashi mo genki deshita. Saikin dou desu ka?
A: Isogashii kedo, tanoshii desu.

MEANING:
A:Hello, long time no see!
B:Hello, it really has been a long time. How have you been?
A:Yes, I've been well. And you?
B:I've been well too. How have you been lately?
A:Busy but enjoying it.

Context: Customer buying something in a store.
Customer:すみません、このシャツはいくらですか？
Clerk:これは三千円です。
Customer:そうですか。じゃあ、これをください。
Clerk:ありがとうございます。袋にお入れしますか？
Customer:はい、お願いします。

Translation:
Customer:Sumimasen, kono shatsu wa ikura desu ka?
Clerk:Kore wa sanzen en desu.
Customer:Sou desu ka. Jaa, kore o kudasai.
Clerk:Arigatou gozaimasu. Fukuro ni o-ire shimasu ka?
Customer:Hai, onegaishimasu.

MEANING:
Customer:Excuse me, how much is this shirt?
Clerk:This is 3000 yen.
Customer:I see. Well, I'll take this.
Clerk:Thank you. Would you like it in a bag?
Customer:Yes, please.

Context: A person asking for directions to the train station.
Person A:すみません、駅はどこですか？
Person B: 駅はあちらです。まっすぐ行って、二つ目の信号を左に曲がってください。
Person A:ありがとうございます。

Translation:
Person A:Sumimasen, kono shatsu wa ikura desu ka?
Person B: Eki wa achira desu. Massugu itte, futatsu-me no shingou o hidari ni magatte kudasai.
Person A:Arigatou gozaimasu.

MEANING:
Person A:xcuse me, where is the train station?
Person B:The station is over there. Go straight and turn left at the second traffic light.
Person A:Thank you.

Context: Ordering food at a restaurant.
Waiter:いらっしゃいませ。何名様ですか？
Customer:二人です。
Waiter:こちらへどうぞ。メニューをどうぞ。
Customer:ありがとうございます。これとこれをください。
Waiter:かしこまりました。

Translation:
Waiter:Irasshaimase. Nan-mei sama desu ka?
Customer:Futari desu.
Waiter:Kochira e douzo. Menyū o douzo.
Customer:Arigatou gozaimasu. Kore to kore o kudasai.
Waiter:Kashikomarimashita.

MEANING:
Waiter:Welcome. How many people?
Customer:Two people.
Waiter:This way, please. Here is the menu.
Customer:Thank you. I'll have this and this.
Waiter:Certainly.

Context: Introducing oneself at a business meeting.
Person A:はじめまして、田中と申します。どうぞよろしくお願いします。
Person B:はじめまして、鈴木です。こちらこそよろしくお願いします。

Translation:
Person A:Hajimemashite, Tanaka to moushimasu. Douzo yoroshiku onegaishimasu.
Person B:Hajimemashite, Suzuki desu. Kochira koso yoroshiku onegaishim

MEANING:
Person A:Nice to meet you, my name is Tanaka. Please treat me well.
Person B:Nice to meet you, I'm Suzuki. Please treat me well too.

Context: Visiting a friend’s home.
Host:いらっしゃい。どうぞお入りください。
Guest:おじゃまします。これ、お土産です。
Host:ありがとうございます。お茶をどうぞ。
Guest:ありがとうございます。

Translation:
Host:Irasshai. Douzo o-hairi kudasai.
Guest:O-jama shimasu. Kore, o-miyage desu.
Host:Arigatou gozaimasu. O-cha o douzo.
Guest:Arigatou gozaimasu.

MEANING:
Host:Welcome. Please come in.
Guest:Excuse me for intruding. This is a small gift.
Host:Thank you. Please have some tea.
Guest:Thank you.

Context: Small talk about the weather.
Person A:今日はいい天気ですね。
Person B:そうですね。暖かくて気持ちいいです。

Translation:
Person A:Kyou wa ii tenki desu ne.
Person B:Sou desu ne. Atatakakute kimochi ii desu.

MEANING:
Person A:It's nice weather today, isn't it?
Person B:Yes, it is. It's warm and pleasant.
